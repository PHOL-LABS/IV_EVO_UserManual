\section{IV-Conf web pack reflashing}\label{app:reflashing}
Use the web pack for firmware updates. Complete the steps below without disconnecting power once the transfer begins.
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Open \url{https://phol-labs.com/iv} and connect to the programmer as described in \autoref{ch:web-config}.
    \item Ensure the baud rate is set to 115200 and click \texttt{Connect MODBUS}. Wait for a green status message.
    \item Press \texttt{Stop Send Data} to halt any automatic preset uploads.
    \item Click the firmware icon, choose the update file supplied with your indicator, and confirm the prompt.
    \item Leave the indicator undisturbed until the progress bar completes and the device restarts.
\end{enumerate}
